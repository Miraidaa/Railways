Game Rules

Welcome to *The Kingdom's Railway Network*! Your objective is to connect the towns of Seeknotland by building a complete railway network across a grid. Plan your routes carefully and compete against the clock to finish the network as fast as possible!

How to Play

  • Choose a difficulty level to set the size of the map:
    • Easy – A 5x5 grid
    • Hard – A 7x7 grid
  • Once the game begins, you’ll see a grid representing different terrain tiles in Seeknotland.
  • Your goal is to place railway tracks to connect all towns within the grid.
  • Each tile can be connected horizontally, vertically, or diagonally based on valid railway placement rules.

Rules for Railway Placement

  • Railways can only be placed on designated tiles. Watch out for blocked or restricted areas!
  • Make sure that your railway connections form a complete path without gaps or dead-ends.
  • The railway network must form a closed loop that connects back to its starting point.

Good luck, and may your trains run smoothly!
